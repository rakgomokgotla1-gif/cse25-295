Safari Destinations in Botswana

Okavango Delta

One of the world’s largest inland deltas. Famous for mokoro canoe safaris, rich birdlife, and large wildlife populations.

Chobe National Park

Home to the largest elephant population in Africa. Visitors enjoy boat cruises along the Chobe River and incredible sunset wildlife viewing.

Moremi Game Reserve

A beautiful reserve within the Okavango Delta. Known for excellent predator sightings including lions, leopards and wild dogs.

Central Kalahari Game Reserve

One of the largest game reserves in the world. Visitors experience vast desert landscapes and unique desert wildlife.

Makgadikgadi Pans

A stunning salt pan landscape. Known for zebra migrations, flamingos, and unique desert safari experiences.

Tsodilo Hills

A UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for ancient rock art and cultural heritage of the San people.
